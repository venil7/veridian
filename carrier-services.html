Carrier Services

Veridian specializes in enabling both new and traditional telephony providers and VoIP operators of any size benefit from the highest quality carrier-to-carrier communications and connectivity anywhere in the world.

Veridian has become an aggressive and quality-driven direct terminator of airtime in the wholesale market which routes millions of minutes through its own switching and network infrastructure and through the major Global Telecom Exchanges and allows other carriers to take advantage of its competitive rates and global buying power.

We service a diverse customer base with multiple carriers, including calling card vendors, retail operators, major wholesalers and a number of PTTs.

Veridian focuses on providing worldwide termination and capacity to meet even the most demanding client requirements.

Veridian Wholesale Voice combines IP with voice services in seamless TDM/IP Telephony package that utilizes our fully meshed MPLS-powered backbone to ensure the highest possible quality for voice traffic backed by our partnerships with major regional and international carriers operating more than 230 active destinations.

We provide high-quality, high-performance, high-capacity, cost-effective carrier-grade International VoIP Termination service to Calling Card Providers, VoIP Resellers, all over the world.
